Get answers to common questions about our process, timeline, and how we work with clients across Texas.

What does an owner's representative do?
An owner's representative sits on your side of the table: coordinating architects, contractors, and consultants; reviewing bids, budgets, and schedules; and preventing scope drift and cost blowouts. We protect your interests, not the project team's margins.
What is a design feasibility audit?
An independent review of your design before you commit money. Feasibility, budget reality, sequencing, and execution risk — delivered as a written memo plus a one-hour readout.
Do you work with developers and builders?
Yes. We bridge designers, builders, developers, and investors who speak different languages — and we've been every one of them. We align the table while sitting firmly on the owner's side.
What areas do you serve?
Dallas-based, all over Texas (Dallas, Austin, Houston, San Antonio), and across the Americas by engagement. We've built in North Carolina, Colorado, Arizona, and Hawaii. Remote audits are routine.

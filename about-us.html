about us

A bridge to an inspired future

We have a vision for the world where humanity re-integrates itself back into harmony with the natural world. A reality where we think consciously about our choices, are liberated to express ourselves freely, and feel empowered to live a life that resonates earnestly with our souls.

We see a future where deep reverence for one another and the world around us is paramount; where we care more about connection than survival. A future where we look at our neighbors with respect and curiosity and make the time to support each other when we are in need.

Our mission at Golman Universal is to design, create, and support the spaces that offer the opportunity for this type of world to exist and thrive.

About Alexander Golman

17 years in outdoor construction. Every role from labor to principal designer. Million-dollar installations, state buildings, universities, multi-property compound developments. I've fought municipalities with engineering data and won. I've taken $50,000 architectural plans that would cost $3 million to execute and redesigned them to fit a $650,000 budget.

Design is my primary expertise. Indoor and outdoor spaces. I see how spaces can work better, flow better, and serve the people who use them.

Estimate & Manage outdoor projects at scale: plants, irrigation, masonry, water features, drainage, hardscape. I've managed crews of 27 people across multiple simultaneous projects.

Connect people to opportunities. When a project has designers, builders, and investors who aren't speaking the same language, I bridge the gap. This happens naturally—it's how I'm wired.
